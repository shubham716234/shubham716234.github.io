1.COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING PUNE

College of Engineering Pune, is a college affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University in Pune, Maharashtra, India. Established in 1854, it is one of the oldest engineering colleges in India, after College of Engineering, Guindy, Chennai and IIT Roorkee. The students and alumni of College of Engineering, Pune are colloquially referred to as COEPians.
